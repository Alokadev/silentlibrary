Books to Read

List of Books

Jungle Book

The Jungle Book is a story about a boy named Mowgli who is raised by wolves in the jungle and has adventures with various animal friends, including a bear named Baloo and a panther named Bagheera.

To Kill a Mockingbird

"To Kill a Mockingbird" is a novel by Harper Lee published in 1960. The book is set in the 1930s in the fictional Maycomb County, Alabama and follows the story of Scout Finch.

The Great Gatsby

The Great Gatsby is a 1925 novel by American writer F. Scott Fitzgerald. Set in the Jazz Age on Long Island, near New York City, the novel depicts first-person narrator Nick Carraway's interactions with mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and Gatsby's obsession to reunite with his former lover, Daisy Buchanan.

Pride and Prejudice

Pride and Prejudice is the story of a young woman in the 1800's, Elizabeth Bennet. The Bennet family has five daughters, and in an age where the only thing women could do was marry rich, all the girls are pressured to find wealthy matches to secure the family's comfort.

Events of Season

A library event is an organized activity or program that takes place within a library or is hosted by a library. These events can include book clubs, author readings, workshops, lectures, and other educational or cultural activities. The purpose of library events is to provide patrons with opportunities to learn, engage with literature and other resources, and connect with their community.

Author Talk Series

A series of events where local and visiting authors come to the library to discuss their work, answer questions, and sign copies of their books.

Literary Festivals

Festivals that celebrate the written word and bring together authors, poets, and other literary figures. These festivals offer the opportunity for attendees to engage with writers, attend readings and signings, and participate in writing workshops.

Career Networking Events

events that connect job seekers with potential employers and recruiters. These events can include resume workshops, mock interviews, and speed networking sessions.
